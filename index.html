    This is the supplementary website for the manuscript titled "Transcriptomics of Bronchoalveolar Lavage Cells Identifies New Molecular Endotypes of Sarcoidosis", where you can download the data, the supervised analysis results, the unsupervised analysis results and the R codes we used to generate the figures in the manuscript here.

    Supervised Analysis

    Supplementary files for the supervised analysis include:

    • Lists of genes associated with clinical traits
    • Pathway enrichment analysis of the genes associated with clinical traits

    Creating pages manually
